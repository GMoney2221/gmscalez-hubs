Backyard Mobile Repair

Backyard Mobile Repair is a mobile mechanic service based in St. Paul, MN that comes to your home, workplace, or wherever your vehicle is sitting. Instead of towing your car to a shop, you can book a visit for brake repair, alternator replacement, serpentine belt replacement, engine replacement, tire changes, or trailer hitch installation. The work gets done on-site by a mechanic who brings the tools and parts to you. It is a practical option for anyone who cannot drive their vehicle or simply does not want to deal with a traditional shop.
